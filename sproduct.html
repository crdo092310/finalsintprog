Plot Summary:

Don Quixote tells the story of a middle-aged nobleman, Alonso Quixano, who becomes obsessed with the chivalric romances of the past. He loses his sanity and adopts the persona of "Don Quixote," a knight-errant who sets out on a series of adventures to revive chivalry and right the world's wrongs. Accompanied by his loyal squire, Sancho Panza, Don Quixote encounters various characters, engages in battles, and faces both humorous and tragic situations.
